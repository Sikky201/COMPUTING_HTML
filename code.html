Metro Boomin

The greatest producer?

Metro Boomin, aka Leland Tyler Wayne, is an American record producer and songwriter.
He is regarded as one of the most influential hip hop and trap music producers.
He frequently collaborates with Future, 21 Savage, Don Toliver, Travis Scott, The Weeknd, Young Thug and etc.

His beats

Metro is famous for many beats, such as Too Many Nights, Annihilate, Niagra Falls and of course, Like That as well as Type Sh*t
Here is the beat for Like That, did you know that this song ignited the diss battle between Drake and Kendrick Lamar?

Other Producers?

There are many other famous producers, such as Pi'erre Bourne, who is best known for his work with Playboi Carti.
